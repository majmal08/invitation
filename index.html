Our Story

The proposal

The proposal was an unexpected twist she never saw coming. A year prior, a proposal had arrived from a family member, but her heart leaned towards academia. I stood by her choice, patient and understanding, recognizing her fervor for her studies. I supported her dreams, waiting as she pursued her degree, never wavering in my respect for her aspirations.

Her appreciation for my kindness and loyalty grew each day, deepening her affection. One day, she saw in me her forever, deciding that I was the one she wanted to journey through life with.

Our engagement became a celebration shared with our loved ones, a prelude to the wedding plans that followed. We made a pact to chase our careers side by side, fueling each other's ambitions and dreams.

Now, as we stand on the brink of our next big step, we are filled with excitement. Our wedding day is approaching, and we eagerly anticipate sharing this moment with you, our cherished family and friends. You have been woven into our story, and we hope you'll join us as we begin this new chapter.
