Our Story

How we met

Our story is not the typical one of love at first sight. In fact, we didn't even know each other before our engagement. Our families arranged our marriage, following the tradition of our culture. We trusted their wisdom and agreed to the proposal, hoping that we would find happiness and compatibility with each other.

We started talking after our engagement, through phone calls and messages. We learned about each other's hobbies, interests, goals, and dreams. We discovered that we had a lot in common, and that we also complemented each other's personalities. We felt a connection and attraction, and we decided to give our relationship a chance.

We have been together for a year now, and we couldn't be happier without each other now. We have supported each other through ups and downs, and we have grown as individuals and as a couple. We have also met each other's families and friends, and we have received their love and blessings.

We are so excited to take the next step in our journey and tie the knot. We can't wait to share our special day with you, our family and friends. You have been a part of our story, and we hope you will join us as we start a new chapter.

The proposal

She got a proposal before a year ago from her family member, but she wanted to take time for her study. I was patient and understanding, and I respected her decision. I knew she was passionate about her education, and I supported her goals and dreams.

I waited for her to finish her degree and I respect her dreams,

She appreciated his kindness and loyalty, and she loved him more every day. She realized I was the one for her, and she wanted to spend the rest of her life with him. She said yes, and I was overjoyed.

We celebrated our engagement with our loved ones, and we started planning our wedding. We also agreed to pursue our careers together, and we supported each other's ambitions and aspirations.

We are so excited to take the next step in their journey and tie the knot. We can't wait to share our special day with you, our family and friends. You have been a part of our story, and they hope you will join them as we start a new chapter.
